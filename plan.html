Best times to come to San Antonio would be during one of the many holidays and festivals, starting with Fiesta Celebrations and Battle of the flowers parades in April, Day of the dead festival in october, and the illuminated river walk on christmas!

Typical weather for san antonio is 80 degrees fahrenheit, but can change with the seasons. Coldest temperatures don't go bellow 30 degrees during winter.

San Antonio has recently added scooters to downtown, and only cost a few cents to use within the downtown area. However please be advised that the city has alcohol restrictions around the river walk area, so please refrain from infringing on them.
